04 The Framework

Four pillars, worked in detail.

01
Empower Youth & Professionals

Build the digital talent pipeline

Goal: Expand Sri Lanka's IT workforce from ~800,000 toward the 2 million target by 2030, with focus on AI, cloud and entrepreneurial skills.

Target groups: University students, IT graduates, mid-career professionals, employees of corporates and SMEs.

Digital Literacy

AI-first WhatsApp microlearning (ENAIBLER) lifting employability at scale.

Advanced Tech

Cloud, AI, cybersecurity & data tracks tied to the $5B export target.

Entrepreneurship

Digital tools for business and access to SL's startup ecosystem.

02
Digital Inclusion & Equity

Bridge Colombo and the districts

Goal: Bring AI skills to women, estate-sector communities and Sri Lanka's 3,100+ under-resourced rural schools.

Target groups: Women re-entering the workforce, rural and estate-sector youth, learners beyond the Western Province.

Online Learning

Sinhala & Tamil microlearning via mobile, on 100% 4G coverage.

Courses for Women

Returnship tracks for working mothers and rural women.

AI for Everyone

Responsible AI literacy and Copilot productivity for everyday roles.

03
Strengthen Digital Ecosystem

Connect local tech to the world

Goal: Link Sri Lanka's tech sector to global opportunities and the diaspora — supporting SLASSCOM's $5B export ambition.

Target groups: SL tech companies, MSMEs, SLASSCOM members, FITIS, and the global diaspora.

Marketplace

A storefront linking SL tech talent and SME services to regional buyers.

Job Creation

Emerging-tech tracks aligned to the 200K skilled-workforce roadmap.

Diaspora Bridge

Brain-gain channels with the 3M-strong diaspora for mentoring & hiring.

04
Supportive Environment

Align policy, schools & industry

Goal: Align with the Digital Economy Blueprint and Ministry of Digital Economy to embed AI capability across schools, universities and the private sector.

Stakeholders: Ministry of Digital Economy, ICTA, Ministry of Education, universities, SLASSCOM, FITIS, corporates.

Policy Advocacy

Engagement with ICTA on the National Digital Economy Strategy 2030.

Institutions

University & NIE partnerships for AI-ready, industry-relevant graduates.

Private Sector

Co-designed training with corporates, banks and telcos.

05 Flagship Program

Meet AIducator.

AIducator

AIducator is ImpactEra's flagship program for Sri Lanka — designed to empower educators with AI-powered tools, Microsoft technologies and practical digital skills to transform teaching, lesson planning and classroom engagement across all 10,047 schools.

  • AI lesson planning with Microsoft Copilot
  • Classroom productivity with Microsoft 365
  • Secure AI usage training in Sinhala, Tamil & English
  • Responsible AI awareness
  • Scalable teacher upskilling, district by district
